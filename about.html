ORIGIN

THESIS

[ FOUNDING_2021 ]

Founded in 2021 in Bengaluru, AIML LABS was born from a fundamental thesis: academic neural intelligence must be translated into robust, deterministic systems for enterprise operation.

We saw that enterprise computing was bottlenecked by generic third-party API dependencies, fragile integrations, and high latency overheads. Our objective was to assemble a world-class deep-tech lab focused on compiling high-dimensional math down to direct, bare-metal efficiency.

Today, we coordinate cognitive infrastructure for major banking, health technology, and defense grids worldwide, operating under zero-trust safety bounds and sub-millisecond lag thresholds.
